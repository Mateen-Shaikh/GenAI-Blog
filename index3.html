Understanding Megatron AI

Megatron AI is a language model architecture developed by NVIDIA, specifically tailored for training massive transformer-based models. The name "Megatron" aptly captures the model's colossal size and computational power. This architecture is built upon the transformer model, a neural network architecture that has proven to be highly effective in natural language processing tasks.
